About

Who is
behind
SheStreams.

I'm Tiauna Hall. I've been in production for 10 years — trained at The Weather Channel, on-set at Fox's Star, and for the last six years running live media for churches and organizations that needed their message to reach further than the room they were standing in.

I built SheStreams because the people I kept working with — speakers, teachers, coaches, creators — all had the same gap. They had something worth saying and no reliable system to get it out. Wrong gear, no workflow, content that went nowhere. I know how to fix all of that, and I built these services to do exactly that.

When you work with SheStreams, you're working with someone who has been in professional production rooms and on broadcast sets, not someone who learned this on YouTube last year. That background is what makes the difference between a session that sounds like advice and one that actually changes what you're putting out.
